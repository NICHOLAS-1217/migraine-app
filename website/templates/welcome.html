Welcome to Migraine Tracker

Your Daily Companion for Migraine Management


Living with migraines can be challenging, but you're not alone. The Migraine Tracker web app is here to support you on your journey to better manage and understand your migraines.

Why Migraine Tracker?


Daily Check-ins
Log your daily experiences to track the frequency and intensity of your migraines. Understanding patterns is the first step towards effective management.

Personalized Insights
Receive personalized insights based on your daily inputs. Identify triggers, potential patterns, and learn more about your migraine patterns over time.

How It Works

Create an Account
Sign up to create your personal Migraine Tracker account. Your data will be securely stored, allowing you to access your information anytime, anywhere.
Daily Check-in
Answer a few quick questions about your day, including migraine frequency, intensity, triggers, and any medications or treatments you've used.
Track Your Progress
Over time, review your migraine history, patterns, and insights generated by the app. Use this information to work towards a healthier, migraine-free lifestyle.

Get Started Today!


Take control of your migraines and start your journey towards a better quality of life. Sign up for Migraine Tracker now and begin tracking your daily experiences

Remember, every data point you log brings you one step closer to understanding and managing your migraines effectively.
